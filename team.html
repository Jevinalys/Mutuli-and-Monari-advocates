Gordon Oduor

Of Counsel
Gordon Oduor
Of Counsel | gordon@mutulimonari.com

Gordon Oduor specialises in Commercial Law and International Dispute Resolution. His legal practice experience spans across East Africa, North America and the Middle East. Gordon is an Advocate of the High Court of Kenya, an Arbitrator, and a Certified Mediator. He is an alumni of Singapore International Arbitration Centre (SIAC) Academy and a member of the ICC Kenya Commission on Arbitration and ADR. Gordon is also an Adjunct Law Lecturer teaching Alternative Dispute Resolution and International Commercial Arbitration at Strathmore Law School. Gordon holds an LL.B. from the University of Nairobi, an LL.M. in International Business Law & Dispute Resolution from the University of Georgia, a Post Graduate Diploma in Law from the Kenya School of Law, and a Diploma in Business Administration from the Institute of Advanced Technology-School of Business.
